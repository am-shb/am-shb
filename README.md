### Hi, I'm Amir 👋

I'm a Tech Lead in Applied AI and Agentic Systems at **PwC Canada**, where I lead a cross-city team building a production multi-agent AI platform on AWS. My work sits at the intersection of LLMs, agentic systems, and production ML: taking research-grade ideas and turning them into systems that ship and hold up under real load.

Before PwC, I was an ML Engineer at the **Canada Revenue Agency**, where I built the agency's first production RAG chatbot. Earlier I was an Applied AI Intern at the **Vector Institute**. At 20 I co-founded **Asan Sports**, Iran's largest sports booking marketplace, as CTO and scaled it to 200K+ users and $1M+ ARR on Kubernetes.

I hold an M.Sc. in Computer Science (AI specialization) with published NLP research, and a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering. More about me at **[shahbandegan.me](https://shahbandegan.me)**.
